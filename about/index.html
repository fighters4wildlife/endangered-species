This project is a collaborative effort of 5th grade students @ Cooper Creek Elementary school as part of the Genius Hour.


It is a place where you can quickly learn about most endangered animals and access resources for contributing to their cause.


By simplifying access to the list of these animals we are hoping to make a difference.
